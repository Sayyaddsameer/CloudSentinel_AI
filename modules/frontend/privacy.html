Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026  ·  We are committed to your privacy

TL;DR: We collect only what's necessary to run the platform. We never sell your data. Your cloud credentials are encrypted. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

🔒 We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

What Data We Collect

Data TypeWhat We CollectWhy
Account Data Name, email address To create and manage your account (stored in Amazon Cognito)
Authentication Hashed passwords, JWT tokens Secure login (managed by AWS Cognito — we never see plaintext passwords)
Cloud Connection AWS account ID, IAM role ARN, GCP project ID To identify which account to scan
GCP Credentials Service account JSON key Encrypted and stored in AWS Secrets Manager — never logged or exposed in frontend
Scan Results Risk metadata (resource names, risk types, remediation steps) To display on your dashboard — stored in DynamoDB in our AWS account
Usage Data Lambda invocation logs (CloudWatch) Debugging and reliability — retained for 30 days then auto-deleted

What We Do NOT Collect

  • The contents of your cloud resources (only metadata about detected risks)
  • Your AWS secret access keys or root credentials
  • Any data from resources we are not explicitly scanning
  • Browser tracking data, cookies for advertising, or fingerprinting

How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session securely
  • Perform cloud security scans on your behalf
  • Display scan results and risk history on your dashboard
  • Send SNS email alerts for high-priority risks (if you opt in)
  • Debug issues and ensure platform reliability

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored within AWS (us-east-1 region) with the following protections:

  • Cognito: User credentials managed by AWS with bcrypt hashing and MFA support
  • DynamoDB: Risk records encrypted at rest with AES-256
  • Secrets Manager: GCP keys encrypted with AWS KMS
  • API Gateway: All endpoints served over HTTPS/TLS 1.2+
  • S3: Artifacts bucket is private with server-side encryption

Third-Party Services

CloudSentinel uses the following AWS services to operate:

  • Amazon Cognito (authentication)
  • Amazon DynamoDB (data storage)
  • AWS Lambda (scan processing)
  • AWS Secrets Manager (credential encryption)
  • Amazon SNS (email notifications)
  • Amazon Bedrock (AI analysis — only when enabled)

All services are covered by AWS's Privacy Policy. We do not use any advertising or analytics third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Your Cognito account and credentials are permanently deleted
  • Scan results in DynamoDB are deleted within 30 days
  • CloudWatch logs are automatically purged after 30 days
  • GCP keys in Secrets Manager are deleted immediately

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correction: Update your name or email in account settings
  • Portability: Export your scan history data on request
  • Withdraw Consent: Disconnect cloud providers at any time from the dashboard

Children's Privacy

CloudSentinel is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you by email at least 14 days before significant changes take effect.
